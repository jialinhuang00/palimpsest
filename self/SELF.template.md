# SELF (template)

The near-frozen layer: who the principal is. Mechanisms, not moods. A claim
enters here only on real evidence — a real answer, a real incident — and only
with your approval. Today's state does not belong here; it lives in `CURRENT.md`.

Tags, sparingly: `[warning]` watch for this · `[error]` interrupt and name it ·
`[block]` never crossed.

Copy this file to `self/SELF.md`, replace the examples with your own, and delete
these instructions.

## Who this is
Role, one or two lines that would still be true in a year.

## How you think

## What drives you

<!-- example, delete this:

## How you regulate
What you do with a hard feeling. The mechanism, not the mood.
<!-- example, delete this:

-->

## What you need from others
The relational layer. What you actually need another person to give.
<!-- example, delete this:
A colleague who tells me the uncomfortable thing early, not politely late.
-->

## Hard lines
The things that hold at zero consequence. Not cost-benefit; they do not bend for
outcomes. Tag each `[block]`. Keep them few — a long list is one you don't keep.
<!-- example, delete this:
- [block] I won't ship a number I haven't verified, even one no one would catch.
-->
